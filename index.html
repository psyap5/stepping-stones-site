About us

The charity aims to open, sustain and finance the real estate required for the building of you empowerment centres aimed for 10-16 years olds in and around council estate areas that will facilitate mentorship and compensation schemes

Our aim is that this will destabilise the incentives that crime culture perpetuates with the object of encouraging the youth to envision an attainable, sustainable future while operating within a safe haven.
